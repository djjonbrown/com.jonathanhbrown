Overview
During my master’s program at CMU’s Human Computer Interaction Institute, I worked with a team of five to explore a prompt from Google Shopping. How Might We empower the salesperson in the omni-channel sales environment, when the shopper has immediate access to all product information. We discovered an emerging trend in in-store shopping, big box stores salespeople were shopping online with the buyer while in-store. We invented Opus, a novel omni-channel experience and the Opus Countertop, an interface to facilitate this new type of interaction.

Experience (UX) Designer

Experience Design requires a broader view of the person than strict product design.  For Google Opus, the product we envisioned was a touch screen that would allow the shopper and the store employee to collaboratively shop at the store's counter. We also envisioned the shopper's experience before engaging with the store employee and conveyed our vision through storytelling.
