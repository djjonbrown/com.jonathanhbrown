As a leader in the design community, I evangelize ethical practices in user experience design. The cybernetic world we envisioned in the early days of the web has devolved into a nudging, divisive corporate panopticon. I am working to return cyberspace to its idealistic roots.

Stop Killer Robots

I am the principal designer at One Sprint Beyond, where we design astonishing simple interfaces at the speed of development. We have mapped our product design and interaction design processes into the cadence of the Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe) to deliver user insights when the business needs them, and interaction design artifacts when engineering needs them.

In other words, we deliver design agility.
